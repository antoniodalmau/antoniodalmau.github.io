<model‑viewer> 3D & AR on the web.

Easily display interactive 3D models on the web & in AR. <model-viewer> is a web component that makes rendering interactive 3D models - optionally in AR - easy to do, on as many browsers and devices as possible.

Easy update. glTF is the "JPEG of 3D".

glTF is a open standard file format for three-dimensional scenes and models. It supports 3D model geometry, appearance, scene graph hierarchy, and animation.
